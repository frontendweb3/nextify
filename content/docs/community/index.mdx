## Contributing to Nextify
Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Nextify! Before making contributions, we recommend reading our `CONTRIBUTING.md` guidelines. This document provides a detailed guide to contributing to nextify.

## Issues
If you're eager to dive in and start contributing immediately, check out the issues in the issue tracker.

## Public Roadmap
If you're wanting to contribute by building out a feature, we recommend taking a look at our #roadmap. This roadmap documents, at a high level, the larger features we're planning to build out in the future.
